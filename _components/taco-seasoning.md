title: "Taco Seasoning"
tags: seasoning, taco, burrito

ingredients:
- 2 Tbsp Chili Powder
- 2 Tbsp Cumin
- 2 Tbsp Black Pepper
- 2 Tbsp Salat
- 2 Tbsp Oregeno
- 2 Tbsp Onion Powder
- 2 Tbsp Garlic Powder
- 1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
- 1 Tbsp Paprika

directions:
- Mix all ingredients together

---

For burrito seasoning you can leave out the chili powder.
