ingredients:
- 1 tablespoon Sour Cream
- 1 tablespoon Greek yogurt, plain
- 1 ea Lime, juiced
- 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
- 1 tsp cumin
- 3 cups cabbage, shredded

directions:
- Mix all ingredients except cabbage
- Pour mix over cabbage and stir
