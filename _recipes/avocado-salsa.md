title: "Avocado Salsa"
image: placeholder.png
tags: appetizer, salad

ingredients:
- 6 ea Avocados, chopped
- 1 can Black Beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 can Corn, drained
- 1/2 ea Red Onion, finely minced
- 1/2 cup Cilantro leaves, chopped
- 1 cup Salsa Verde
- 1/2 ea Lime, juiced
- 1/2 tsp Salt

directions:
- Mix in large bowl
- Chill before serving
- Enjoy
