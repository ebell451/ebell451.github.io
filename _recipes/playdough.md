---

layout: recipe
title: "Playdough"
tags: fun

ingredients:
- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup salt
- 1 cup water
- 1 T vegetable oil
- colors of your choosing

directions:
- Mix first four ingredients and knead for 5 minutes.
- Split into 4 equal sections.
- Add food coloring.
- Knead for 5 minutes, wearing gloves.
